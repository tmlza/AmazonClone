Reviewed in the United States on October 23, 2023
Verified Purchase

Product is great. Does exactly as it is described. Help turn your gpu in to an E-gpu. Device must be thunderbolt 3 or 4 compatible. After building this set up I instantly saw gains in fps and benchmarking. Whole build cost less than name brand enclosures alone. Ran out of funds but would love to get there enclosures to complete.


This is perfect for laptops that have a decent amount of RAM, good processor, and lacking in the gpu department. You use your laptop for your daily grind and get home plug in the thunderbolt and game to relieve some of that stress.
